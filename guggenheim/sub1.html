During Your Visit

Exhibitions

Discover the relationship between line, space, and volume through Gego’s work explore how Sarah Sze investigates the way we experience time and place in our digitally and materially saturated world learn how Korean artists harnessed the power of contemporary art to explore pressing issues in an era of remarkable transformation and enjoy Impressionist, Post-Impressionist, and modern French masterpieces from the Guggenheim's permanent collection in these must-see exhibitions.

Café Rebay

Open Monday–Sunday, 11 am–3 pm

Relax and enjoy a latté, a glass of wine, or a snack while taking in views of Central Park. Café Rebay serves pastries and chocolates, fresh sandwiches and salads, as well as Lavazza coffee, tea, wine, and beer.
